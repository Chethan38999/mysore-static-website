Popular Places in Mysore

Place Description
Mysore Palace The magnificent Mysore Palace, also known as the Amba Vilas Palace, is a stunning example of Indo-Saracenic architecture and is a must-visit attraction in Mysore.
Chamundi Hill Chamundi Hill is home to the Chamundeshwari Temple, dedicated to the Goddess Chamundeshwari. Visitors can climb the 1,000 steps to reach the temple and enjoy panoramic views of Mysore from the top.
Brindavan Gardens The Brindavan Gardens, located near the Krishnarajasagara Dam, is famous for its musical fountain and beautifully landscaped gardens. It's a great place to relax and enjoy the evening.
St. Philomena's Church St. Philomena's Church is a neo-gothic style cathedral and one of the largest churches in India. It's known for its stunning architecture and peaceful ambiance.
